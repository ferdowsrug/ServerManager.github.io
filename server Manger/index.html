Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

Server Menger will only collect data from servers that have certain features enabled. To opt out of your data being collected, keep these features disabled. There is only one person whom will see your collected data, my developer: Ferdows_Rug. Your data will not be used other than to make your server a fun place to be.
